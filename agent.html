Your Master Guide to Property Buying

Navigate the complex real estate market with confidence. Follow our step-by-step timeline covering expert tips, legalities, and financing.

Download Full Guide
Step 01

Expert Market Tips

Start by researching the neighborhood. Look for upcoming infrastructure projects, school districts, and historical price trends. Always negotiate based on market data, not emotions.

  • Analyze 5-year price trends
  • Hire a certified home inspector
Read Tips
Step 02

Legal & Documentation

Ensure the property has a clear title. Verify the encumbrance certificate (EC), approved building plans, and property tax receipts before making any token advance.

  • Check 30-year parent documents
  • Verify RERA registration
View Checklist
Step 03

Home Loan & Financing

Don't just look at the interest rate. Compare processing fees, pre-closure charges, and floating vs. fixed rates. Always get a pre-approved loan letter before house hunting.

  • Maintain a CIBIL score of 750+
  • Compare minimum 3 bank offers
Compare Loans
Step 04

Final Investment Checklist

Calculate your gross rental yield and expected ROI. Factor in maintenance costs, property taxes, and potential vacancy periods before finalizing the deal.

  • Calculate Net Operating Income
  • Plan your exit strategy
